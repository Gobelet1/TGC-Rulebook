How to use the Ticket system & the rules.

The ticket tool can be used for a variety of things, but its main purpose is to contact staff. Upon use, a new channel will be created which is accessible to only you and the staff members. There are plenty of reasons to open a ticket, but the main ones are:

  • Becoming a partner or affiliate with our server.
  • Requesting role perks for server boosting and/or platform roles.
  • Submitting a report about a member.
  • Any help you may need regarding the server.
  • To apply for staff positions, if applications are open.

  • Respect everyone.
  • No NSFW.
  • Promotion and sharing.
  • Pings and DM's.
  • TOS and topics.
  • Tickets and their use.
  • Important Channels.
  • Additional Information.

You are not allowed to open a ticket for any other (invalid) reasons. Suggestions, boredom, false reports and/or false evidence for a report, are all prohibited and will result in a warning or ban. Even for a misclick, this still applies, and is non-arguable.
