Rules Regarding Discord's TOS, topics and languages.

  • Respect everyone.
  • No NSFW.
  • Promotion and sharing.
  • Pings and DM's.
  • TOS and topics.
  • Tickets and their use.
  • Important Channels.
  • Additional Information.

Discord's TOS must always be followed. This is a requirement both for us as a server and for you as a member. One of the most important rules included in that is that you have to be 13+ in order to join this server, and if we find out that's not the case, you will be kicked or banned for at least a year. You are also not allowed to provide access to hacking, cracked, or stolen software as per TOS.

Every channel has a topic, as you can see by its name, and you must stick to this topic. If a discussion gradually leads you off-topic, that is okay, but dont start a discussion on the wrong topic. Staff may tell you to change channel or end a topic entirely in certain cases.
English is the only allowed language in the server, for every channel, including spoken messages or voice channels. However, small messages in a different language are allowed under the following circumstances:
>You dont know a word in English and want to translate it to or from your language.
>You provide an English description of your message (E.G. when referencing something that doesn't exist in English).
